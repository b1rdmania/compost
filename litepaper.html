The problem

HYPE holders want yield. HIP-3 is fragmented.

HIP-3 unlocked a new yield source: trading fees from builder markets. But accessing it is hard:

  • Direct deployment requires 500K HYPE and operational capacity
  • Pooled programs vary by market — different terms, different tokens, different risks
  • LP vaults carry strategy risk and require active monitoring
  • Stake provider deals require relationships and negotiation

Most HYPE holders either stake with validators (~2.5%) or sit on the sidelines watching HIP-3 grow without them.

HIP-3 mechanisms

How we access builder fee yield

HIP-3 markets require 500K HYPE to deploy. The builder earns ~50% of trading fees. We access this yield through multiple mechanisms:

Mechanism Example Growth Mode Standard Mode
Pooled wrapper Kinetiq kmHYPE 0.5-2% 4-8%
LP vault Ventuals VLP -5% to +15% -5% to +20%
Stake provider Felix arrangements 0.5-2% 5-10%
Collateral yield HyENA USDe 5-15% 5-15%
Direct deployment Phase 2+ 1-3% 8-15%+

Now: Pooled wrappers and LP vaults. Collateral yield where it makes sense.

Later: Direct deployment when we have scale (500K+ HYPE).

$32B+ traded. 10x upside when fees normalise.
