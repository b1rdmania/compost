About the paper below

What follows is the September 2025 concept paper — the original design document for cHYPE. It is the cleanest single-source statement of the thesis (capital formation for HIP-3 builder markets, fee pool, founding-syndicate framing) and useful background for HL-native operators and depositors. It is not a description of a live product — see the demos above for what's actually been built, and the homepage for current framing. Pair it with the cHYPE yield design for the math.
