# Who It's For

Compost serves different users with different needs.

---

## User Types

### HYPE Holders

**Use case:** Earn yield without picking markets

You hold HYPE. You want it working for you. But you don't have $15M, and you don't want to research every deployer.

Compost gives you diversified HIP-3 exposure in a single transaction.

---

### DeFi Users

**Use case:** Composable yield token

cHYPE is an ERC-20 token. Use it:

| Platform | Use Case |
|----------|----------|
| Pendle | Split into PT + YT, trade future yield |
| Lending protocols | Collateral, borrow against position |
| DEXs | LP in cHYPE pairs |
| Yield aggregators | Auto-compound strategies |

---

### Institutions

**Use case:** Compliant access to HIP-3 yield

Funds, family offices, and platforms need:
- KYC/AML onboarding
- NAV reporting
- Custody integration
- Dedicated support

Compost provides institutional infrastructure for HIP-3 exposure.

---

### Protocols

**Use case:** White-label yield infrastructure

Platforms can integrate Compost yield for their users:
- API access
- Custom branding
- Revenue share arrangements

---

## Summary

| User | Need | Solution |
|------|------|----------|
| HYPE holders | Passive yield | Deposit and earn |
| DeFi users | Composability | Use cHYPE in protocols |
| Institutions | Compliance | KYC, reporting, custody |
| Protocols | Infrastructure | API, white-label |
