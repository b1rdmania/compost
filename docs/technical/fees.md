# Fees

Compost fee structure.

---

## Protocol Fee

**15% of all yield** (staking + builder fees)

Deducted from yield before compounding into vault.

---

## What's Included

| Fee Type | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Deposit fee | None |
| Withdrawal fee | None |
| Management fee | None |
| Performance fee | 15% of yield |

---

## Fee Split

| Recipient | Share | Use |
|-----------|-------|-----|
| Compost operations | 90% | Team, growth, marketing, legal, audit |
| Infrastructure partner | 10% | Vault build and maintenance |

---

## Example

```
Vault generates 100 HYPE in yield

Protocol fee: 15 HYPE (15%)
  ├── Compost operations: 13.5 HYPE (90%)
  └── Infrastructure partner: 1.5 HYPE (10%)

Net yield to vault: 85 HYPE (85%)
```

---

## Launch Bonus

{% hint style="success" %}
**First 30 days: 0% protocol fee**

All yield goes to stakers during launch period. Standard 15% fee begins on day 31.
{% endhint %}

---

Institutional clients may negotiate different terms based on:
