Validator staking is the safe baseline. We stake HYPE with Hyperliquid validators. The rate is set by the protocol — roughly 2.5% at current stake levels.

HIP-3 strategies are where most yield comes from. Different markets, different structures. More on this below.

Liquidity buffer earns nothing. It's there so you can withdraw without waiting for unstaking queues.

HIP-3 yield

What "HIP-3" actually means

HIP-3 is Hyperliquid's system for new trading markets. Someone stakes 500,000 HYPE to deploy a market. In return, they get a share of the trading fees — typically 50%.

That's the core primitive: stake HYPE → earn trading fees.

But there are different ways to access this yield. Here are the five we've identified:

Mechanism 1
Direct deployment
We stake 500K HYPE ourselves. We operate the market. We keep 50% of trading fees. Highest yield, highest effort.
Mechanism 2
Stake provider
Someone else operates the market. We provide the stake. We split fees with them. Less work, less yield.
Mechanism 3
Pooled wrapper
A program pools capital from many stakers. The pool has rights to market revenue. We get our share.
Mechanism 4
LP vault
A vault provides liquidity to a HIP-3 market. Yield comes from trading profits, not fee share. Can go negative.
Mechanism 5
Collateral yield
Some markets use reward-bearing collateral (like USDe). Yield comes from the collateral, not the trading fees.
Our approach

We use a mix. Pooled wrappers and LP vaults are available now. Stake provider deals require negotiation. Direct deployment requires 500K HYPE and operational capacity — that's Phase 2.

The catch

Growth Mode means low fees — for now

Most HIP-3 markets run in "Growth Mode." This cuts trading fees by 90% to attract volume. Good for traders. Bad for yield.

The difference
Standard fees:
$32B volume × 4.5 bps = $14.4M total fees
Builder share (50%) = $7.2M to stakers

Growth Mode fees:
$32B volume × 0.45 bps = $1.44M total fees
Builder share (50%) = $0.72M to stakers

Difference: 10x less yield in Growth Mode

When will Growth Mode end? We don't know. Markets decide individually. Some may stay in Growth Mode for months. Some may switch soon.

Our yield projections assume most markets stay in Growth Mode for now. If they switch, our yield goes up. We're not betting on that.

Our cut

What we charge — and when

Fee Amount When it applies
Deposit fee 0% Never
Withdrawal fee 0% Never
Management fee 0.5%/year On HIP-3 allocation only
Performance fee 15% Only on returns above staking rate

Management fee is 0.5% annually on the HIP-3 portion. If HIP-3 is 50% of the vault, your effective fee is ~0.25% of your total deposit.

Performance fee only kicks in when we beat vanilla HYPE staking (~2.5%). If vault yields 8%, we take 15% of the 5.5% excess — that's 0.825%. If we underperform staking, we take nothing.

Example: 8% vault yield
Vault yield: 8%
HYPE staking rate: 2.5%
Excess return: 5.5%

Management fee: 0.5% × 50% (HIP-3 portion) = 0.25%
Performance fee: 15% × 5.5% = 0.825%

Total fees: ~1.08%
Your net yield: ~6.92%
Example: 3% vault yield (underperforming)
Vault yield: 3%
HYPE staking rate: 2.5%
Excess return: 0.5%

Management fee: 0.5% × 50% = 0.25%
Performance fee: 15% × 0.5% = 0.075%

Total fees: ~0.33%
Your net yield: ~2.67%

Summary

What you need to remember

Yield comes from two places
Validator staking (~2.5%) and HIP-3 strategies (variable). The mix depends on market conditions.
Growth Mode limits yield now
Most HIP-3 markets run at 90% reduced fees. When that changes, yield improves. We don't know when.
We only win when you win
Performance fee only applies above the staking rate. If we underperform vanilla staking, we earn almost nothing.
Be realistic

In the near term, expect modest yields (3-7%). If HIP-3 markets take off and exit Growth Mode, yields could be much higher. We're building for that future, but we're not promising it.
